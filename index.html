Kids Love to Read!

Teachers say it starts at home! You should make it fun for children to Read. and not an obligation. Every kid learns and reads differently. Nowadays children are on their IPads for most of the day. Things can't go fast enough. So we should learn them that it is good to take their time. That reading is fun and that it is good for the imagination. They learn how to read, be imaginative and can learn a lot from characters and social behavior from books. So how can we make it Fun again? DON'T make it an obligation. Create an atmosphere where children see reading as a treat. Tell stories from the bottum of our heart. In stead of a must before bedtime. Make it fun for yourself. Children copy behavior. Come up with own stories, tell them to your kids and let your kids create stories, they will probably suck in the beginning, but it could also be very funny. TEST
